AI automation is the use of artificial intelligence technologies to perform tasks without human intervention. It involves leveraging machine learning algorithms, robotics, and other AI-driven tools to streamline processes and increase efficiency across various industries. This can include automating routine tasks, enhancing decision-making, and improving overall productivity.

Common AI workflow examples are data extraction and analysis, text translation and summarization, customer support automation, image classification and processing, content generation, and sentiment analysis.
